PHOTOGRAPHY

Photography and Advertisement Campaign

The course assignment involves the creation of an advertisement for a fictional client of our choice. The photography should form the basis of the advertising campaign for the product. We were required to conceptualize and plan a creative photo shoot and produce the final advertisement for the chosen product. Also, we are asked to ensure that the photograph incorporates model photography in combination with either landscape or architectural photography in a visually captivating way. We are required to complete the following:
1. Produce one or more high-quality conceptual photograph(s) advertising a product or service
2. Create an advertisement using the photograph you produced
3. The photograph needs to do the talking by keeping the layout basic
4. All photographs used must be self-produced. No pre-produced backgrounds or images may be used
5. The final advertisement must be submitted as a high-quality A4 PDF
