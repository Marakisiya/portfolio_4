SEMESTER EXAM 2

Noor Braiding Salon

The project exam involves the design and implementation of graphic design materials for business client called Noor Braiding Salon. The client wants brand identity and online presence in the digital world in order to promote its businesses. Thus, in this project I will be producing the following touchpoints for the client:
• Brand Identity Development (logo, color scheme, typography)
• Website (home, hairstyle gallery, booking, contact us pages)
• Social Media Profile setup (with 2 or three posts to show how the brand will roll out)
• Billboard design (2 sizes)
• Flyer
• Business Card
• Mock-ups of miscellaneous items (t-shirts, mugs etc).
