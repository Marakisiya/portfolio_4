Watch Out Motion Design

The course assignment involves the creation of an amazing motion design of our choice that could impress potential employers and clients. We asked to develop the full motion design from concept to export taking into consideration all the lessons learned from the following module assignments:
1. How to create a concept and rough sketches of the motion design project
2. How to create illustration of the idea and concept of the motion design project
3. How to create a storyboard of all the illustrations of the motion design project
4. How to create effects and keyframing on motion design project
5. How to animate the motion design project For this course assignment and using all the skills gain from the above-mentioned lesson module, I decided to create a motion design to show the danger that can occur when people lack concentration and pay less attention when they walk in public space. The title of my motion design is Watch Out!!
