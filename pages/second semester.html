website design

In this project, I design and deployed a website for the fictional new poultry farm business. The task include designing a logo for to showcase the business brand. The business was established with the aim of delivering high quality livestock products at affordable prices to consumers. The farm produces eggs, chickens, and chicks. The business wants to come across as a growing poultry farm that produces quality, safe and nutritious livestock products for consumers at affordable prices. The audience are the general public (male and female) from the age of 16 and above. The website needs to showcase the products and provide customers with relevant information about the business and allows online ordering of products. I design a logo for the business, which create a brand identity for the business. The PDF copy of the logo can be view here. I design the website to contain home, product, about us, and contact pages. Customers will be able read about the product, order them online and see their order in a shopping cart. The design allows for alot of interactivity and navigation.
